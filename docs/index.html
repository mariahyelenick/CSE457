Welcome to Wanna Date-a?

Are you looking for a significant other? Have you ever wondered if OkCupid is the platform that will best suit your needs? Maybe you're just curious what kind of people are on dating sites? If so, we hope that our data visualization can help you! Our visualization analyzes around 22,000 OkCupid profiles.

How to use

Start your interaction by dragging the categories below, placing your higher priority categories at the top. The top three categories are taken to generate the sunburst. Hover over any slice of the sunburst to see which attribute you are investigating. Click on any slice to explore the people who fit those attributes.
  • Sex
  • Age bucket
  • Has pets
  • Height
  • Ethnicity
  • Smokes
  • Drinks
  • Does drugs
  • Body type
  • Diet
  • Orientation
  • Actual religion
  • Zodiac sign
